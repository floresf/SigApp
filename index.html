How to paste your signature into Mail.app and Kerio

Follow the directions below.
Step 1: Copy your generated signature to your clipboard.

Mail.app Instructions

Step 2: Open Mail.app, navigate to Mail > Preferences... in the menubar. Then click on "Signatures" in the top bar. Paste your signature into a new signature.

Please note: It's normal for the logo image to show up as a question mark initially.

Kerio Instructions

Step 2: Open kerio.macexp.com. After logging in, go to your user name and click "Settings"

Step 3: Paste your signature into the box. (remember to click save!)
